## [KR] 투자자용 쉬운 설명서

### 종자감자 세대(G0-G3)란 무엇인가?

감자는 씨앗이 아닌 종자감자로 번식한다. 각 세대는 다음과 같다:

G0 (기초 종자): 실험실에서 조직배양으로 생산된 최초의 무균 미세괴경(PMT). 크기 5-10g, 100% 무균, 비GMO. Bloombase 기술의 핵심 출발점이다.

G1 (1세대 증식): G0 미세괴경을 온실에서 재배하여 생산. 조직배양 식물체 1개당 최소 30개의 괴경이 생산된다. 고순도 상태를 유지한다.

G2 (2세대 증식): G1을 포장(field)에서 대량 재배하여 생산. 상업적 규모의 증식이 시작되는 단계이다.

G3 (농가 공급용): 농가에 최종 판매되는 종자감자. Bloombase의 주요 매출원이다. 시장가 대비 15% 저렴하게 공급하여 농가 채택을 극대화한다.

### 왜 바이러스가 문제이고, 무균 조직배양이 중요한가?

감자는 세대를 거듭할수록 바이러스가 축적된다. 바이러스 감염 종자감자를 사용하면 수확량이 30-50% 감소하고, 감자 크기와 품질이 저하된다. 농가는 이를 인지하지 못한 채 감염된 종자를 계속 사용하는 악순환에 빠진다.

Bloombase의 PMT 기술은 이 사이클을 완전히 차단한다. 조직배양 과정에서 바이러스가 100% 제거된 무균 식물체를 생산하며, 이로부터 증식된 G1-G3 종자감자는 최고 수준의 순도를 유지한다. 98% 이상의 생존율은 이 기술의 안정성을 입증한다.

## [EN] Investor-Friendly Explainer

### What Is Seed-Potato Generation (G0-G3)?

Potatoes reproduce not from seeds but from seed potatoes. Each generation is defined as follows:

G0 (Foundation Seed): The initial sterile micro-tubers (PMT) produced via tissue culture in a laboratory. Size 5-10g, 100% sterile, non-GMO. This is the core starting point of Bloombase technology.

G1 (First-Generation Multiplication): Produced by growing G0 micro-tubers in greenhouses. Each tissue-culture plant yields a minimum of 30 tubers. High purity is maintained.

G2 (Second-Generation Multiplication): Produced by mass-cultivating G1 in open fields. This is where commercial-scale multiplication begins.

G3 (Farm-Supply Grade): The seed potatoes sold to farmers. This is Bloombase's primary revenue source. Priced at 15% below market price to maximize farmer adoption.

### Why Viruses Matter and Why Sterile Tissue Culture Is Critical

Potatoes accumulate viruses with each successive generation. Using virus-infected seed potatoes reduces harvest yields by 30-50% and degrades tuber size and quality. Farmers often remain unaware and perpetuate this cycle by replanting infected stock.

Bloombase PMT technology breaks this cycle entirely. The tissue-culture process produces 100% virus-free sterile plantlets; all G1-G3 seed potatoes derived from them maintain the highest purity levels. Survival rates above 98% validate the technology reliability.

- Contractual claims to revenue distributions from the Bloombase project
- Entitlement to periodic distributions enforced via smart contracts
- Access to monthly operational and financial reports
- Right to transfer tokens within the KYC-approved whitelist (secondary trading)

Not Owned:
- Direct ownership of physical assets (farms, laboratories, equipment)

Senior Tranche (BMPT-SR): 전체 모집액의 60%를 차지하며, 수익 배분에서 최우선 순위를 갖는다. 고정 쿠폰(연 8-10%, 가정)이 매 분기 지급된다. 생산이 목표에 미달하더라도 Reserve Fund와 Junior 트랜치의 후순위 흡수를 통해 보호된다. 안정적인 수익을 선호하는 투자자에 적합하다.

Junior Tranche (BMPT-JR): 전체 모집액의 40%를 차지하며, Senior 쿠폰 지급 후 잔여 수익을 배분받는다. Senior 대비 높은 기대수익률(연 14-20%, 가정)을 가지나, 첫 손실(First-Loss)을 부담한다. 생산이 목표를 초과할 경우 초과 수익의 대부분을 향유한다. 높은 수익-위험 프로파일을 수용하는 투자자에 적합하다.

Ramp-Up Period (Month 1-19): 초기 19개월은 시설 건설 및 첫 생산 사이클 기간이다. 이 기간 동안 Senior에는 최소 쿠폰(연 4%, 가정)이 발생 기준으로 적립되며, 첫 수확 매출 발생 시점부터 정상 쿠폰이 지급된다. Junior는 ramp-up 기간 동안 배분이 없다.

During this period, Senior accrues a minimal coupon (assumed 4% p.a.) on an accrual basis, with full coupon commencing upon first harvest revenue. Junior receives no distributions during ramp-up.

## [KR] 수익 배분 구조

### 지급 워터폴 (Payout Waterfall)

매 분기 매출이 발생하면, 다음 순서로 배분된다:

1단계 - 운영비 충당: 해당 분기의 농장 운영비, 인건비, 원재료비 등 직접 비용을 우선 공제한다.

2단계 - 유지보수 적립금(Maintenance Reserve): 분기 순매출의 5%를 시설 유지보수 및 긴급 비용 대응을 위해 적립한다.

3단계 - 원리금상환적립금(DSRA-like Buffer): 다음 2개 분기의 Senior 쿠폰 지급액에 해당하는 금액을 상시 유지한다. 부족 시 이 단계에서 보충한다.

4단계 - Senior 쿠폰 지급: Senior 토큰 보유자에게 고정 쿠폰(연 8-10% 가정)을 분기별로 지급한다.

5단계 - Junior 수익 배분: Senior 쿠폰 지급 및 적립금 충족 후 잔여 수익을 Junior 토큰 보유자에게 반기별로 배분한다.

6단계 - 성과보수 공제: Junior 수익률이 연 15% 허들을 초과할 경우, 초과분의 10%가 성과보수로 공제된다.

Maintenance Reserve: 분기 순매출의 5%를 적립하며, 상한은 총 모집액의 10%이다. 상한 도달 시 적립 중단하고 Junior 배분으로 전환한다.

DSRA Buffer: 최소 다음 2개 분기 Senior 쿠폰 지급액을 상시 유지한다. 이 금액은 별도 에스크로 계좌에 보관되며 스마트 계약으로 관리된다.

## [EN] Payout Scheme

### Payout Waterfall

Each quarter when revenue is generated, distributions follow this order:

Step 1 - Operating Cost Coverage: Direct costs for the quarter (farm operations, labor, raw materials) are deducted first.

Step 2 - Maintenance Reserve: 5% of quarterly net revenue is set aside for facility maintenance and emergency costs.

Step 3 - DSRA-like Buffer: An amount equal to the next 2 quarters of Senior coupon payments is maintained at all times. Any shortfall is replenished at this step.

Step 4 - Senior Coupon Payment: Fixed coupon (assumed 8-10% p.a.) paid quarterly to Senior token holders.

Step 5 - Junior Distribution: Residual revenue after Senior coupon and reserve fulfillment is distributed to Junior token holders semi-annually.

Step 6 - Performance Fee Deduction: If Junior return exceeds the 15% p.a. hurdle, 10% of excess is deducted as performance fee.

### Reserve Policy

Maintenance Reserve: 5% of quarterly net revenue, capped at 10% of total raise. Upon reaching cap, accrual ceases and redirects to Junior distribution.

DSRA Buffer: Minimum next 2 quarters of Senior coupon maintained at all times in a segregated escrow account managed via smart contract.

### Ramp-Up Period Behavior

Month 1-19 (Construction and First Cycle): Senior accrues at 4% p.a. (assumption). No cash payment until first harvest revenue, at which point accumulated accrual is paid in lump sum. Junior receives no distributions.

Month 20 onward: Normal waterfall commences. Senior paid quarterly, Junior distributed semi-annually.

# SECTION 7: SMART CONTRACT / ON-CHAIN SPEC (EN)

## Token Architecture

### Token Types
BMPT-SR (Senior): ERC-20 token on Ethereum (or Polygon/Base L2). Represents a proportional claim on Senior tranche revenue distributions. Each token equals one unit of participation in the Senior tranche.

BMPT-JR (Junior): ERC-20 token. Represents a proportional claim on Junior tranche residual distributions. Bears first-loss risk.

### Transfer Restriction Rules
Whitelist-Only Transfers: Both tokens implement a transfer restriction where sender AND recipient must be on a KYC-verified whitelist managed by the Issuer. Transfers to non-whitelisted addresses revert.

Region Restrictions: Addresses associated with OFAC-sanctioned jurisdictions are permanently blocked. The whitelist contract supports jurisdiction flags per address.

Lockup Enforcement: Senior tokens are non-transferable for 12 months post-issuance. Junior tokens are non-transferable for 18 months. After lockup, transfers are permitted within the whitelist.

### Payout Function Logic
Snapshot-Based Distribution: On each distribution date, a snapshot of token holder balances is taken at a predetermined block number.

Stablecoin Distribution: Payouts are denominated and distributed in USDC (or USDT). The distribution contract holds the stablecoin amount and allows token holders to claim their pro-rata share.

Claim Mechanism: Holders call a claim() function to receive their distribution. Unclaimed distributions remain available for 180 days, after which they are swept to the Reserve.

Waterfall Logic: The payout contract enforces the waterfall: OpEx escrow first, then Maintenance Reserve, then DSRA top-up, then Senior coupon, then Junior distribution. Each step is a conditional check on-chain.

### Admin and Emergency Controls
Pause Function: An admin multisig (3-of-5) can pause all token transfers and distribution claims in the event of a security incident or regulatory action.

Upgradability: Contracts use a transparent proxy pattern (UUPS) to allow bug fixes while preserving state. All upgrades require multisig approval and are subject to a 48-hour timelock.

Whitelist Management: Only the designated compliance officer address (controlled by multisig) can add or remove addresses from the whitelist.

Audit Trail: All admin actions (pause, unpause, whitelist changes, upgrades) emit events that are permanently recorded on-chain.

### Oracle Inputs Needed
Sales Receipt Oracle: Quarterly, an authorized reporter submits a hash of the signed sales receipts to the on-chain registry. This hash can be verified against the original document in the data room.

Bank Attestation Oracle: Quarterly, the escrow bank provides a signed attestation of account balances. The hash is recorded on-chain.

Production Volume Oracle: Monthly, production volumes are submitted by the operations team and countersigned by the semi-annual auditor.

Note: Until fully decentralized oracle infrastructure is available for agricultural RWAs, these oracles operate on a trusted-reporter model with multisig and audit controls.
